Chapter 1 Making Measurements

1.1 length&volume

  1. 测量工具：
    1） ruler 尺子 （e.g. metre ruler)
    测量注意点：（以wire为例）【4步】
  • straight，laid closely alongside
  • end: cut neatly/ragged
  • line one end of the wire up against the “0”
  • look at the other end and read
    一定要注意测出来的是否可以保证正确2）游标卡尺 螺旋测微器
    游标：L=整的毫米数（主尺）+0.1*n毫米（游标）
  1. 测量方法
    1）measure sth. thin
    以测纸的厚度为例：【3步】
  • take a stack of 500 sheets
  • measure its thickness
  • devide by 5002）measure sth. curved
    以测弯曲的铁条为例：【3步】
  • lay a thread along the line
  • mark the thread at either end
  • lay it along a ruler3）measuring volumes
    i. cube/cuboid
    【3要素】
    • length
    • width
    • height
      =multiply
      other regular shapes(spheres…):
      look up the equation for the volume
    ii. liquids
    measuring cylinder 量筒
    beaker 烧杯
    look at the scale horizontally and read the level of the bottom of the meniscus(简单表达：eyes level with the scale)
    用最合适的量筒。e.g. 你不能用5ml的量筒测10mliii. measure bu displacement (for irregular objects) 【6步】

    • note V1

    • note V2
    • Vobject=V2-V1
  1. 国际单位：
    SI units：metre（m)
    1l=1dm^3 1ml=1cm^3
    1deci(dm): 0.1m 1centi(cm):0.01m
    1mili(mm):0.001m 1micro(um):0.000001m
    1kilo~(km):1000m

1.2 Density

  1. 公式：
    Density (ρ)=Mass (m)/Volume (V)
    做题时m和V两个要素缺一不可
  2. SI units: kg/m^3
  3. 密度的性质：
    1）Gas<solid/liquid
    2）任何在水面漂浮的物体密度都比水小。（e.g.冰）
    3）许多物质都有不同的密度（e.g.木头）
    4）水的密度：1*10^3kg/m^3, 1g/cm^3
    5）immicible不溶中最上层的液体密度最小

    digital(数字）clock (most precise）
  2. 测量方法：
    测量短时间：e.g.plumb bob单摆

Chapter 2 Describing Motion

2.1 Understanding Speed

  1. 公式：
    Speed(average)(v)=Total Distance(s)/​Total Time(t)
  2. SI units: m/s
  3. Speed in the laboratory
    light gate
    法1:
    • start the timer: peg attached to the trolley breaks the beam of one of the light gate
    • stop the timer: breaks the second timer
      法2：
    • mount an interrupt card(遮光片) on the trolley
    • start the timer: the leading edge of the interrupt card breaks the beam
    • stop the timer: the trailing edge passed the gate

2.3 Understanding Acceleration

  1. 定义：
    acceleration: 加速度
    the rate at which its speed is changing/the change in speed per unit time
  2. 性质：
    vector矢量 e.g.velocity
    scalar标量 e.g.speed
  3. 图像：
    speed-time graph
    • the steeper the slope, the greater the acceleration
    • a negative slope means a deceleration
    • a horizontal graph(slope=0) meeans a constant speed
      distance travelled=area under speed-time graph
