IGCSE ESL Full Mark Movie Review

Posted on 2022-03-02 Edited on 2022-06-25 In IGCSE Views:
A full mark movie review for IGCSE ESL Writing

Task:
Your class is starting a film club for all students. Your Head of English would like students to submit reviews about films they would recommend for the club. Reviews will be published in the school newsletter.
Write a review about a film you would recommend for the school film club.

Model Answer(16/16):

Cruella
Have you ever seen the Disney made the villian the main character in its film? Most people only know those kind-hearted heroines, like Cinderella and Snow White. However, Cruella, directed by Craig Gillespie, a Disney film, tells a different story.
Set in the storyline of the 101 Dalmatians, the lead character is in fact the villian Cruella De Vil who has black and white hair. Her real name is Estella, and she is raised by a very kind but poor mother. But even though she has a mother like a princess from Disney, her dark side, Cruella, played by Emma Stone, grows bigger and bigger. By accident, Estella’s talent in designing clothes is discovered by the most famous fashion designer Baroness von Hellman. However, she later finds out that the designer is actually the one who killed her mother. I won’t spoil whether she will use her darkside to revenge or not.
The film has a quirky plot and many memorable scenes. When Estella is designing, the clothes are so gorgeous that everyone in the movie theater gasped. Every move of the film is essential and not too many songs are contained in the show, which is a success for Disney movies. It also has a serious message, that not only the princesses and kind people can be the heroes, but everyone can be a hero to their lives.
“I’m a woman, hear me roar.”This has become many people’s quote since Cruella came out. This is a masterpiece in films and is a turning point for Disney movies. Students in the film club and those who like films should find it particularly intersting.


About:
This is a movie review I wrote as my month-end test. In the test, all the names included above were all made up, but that didn’t affect my score. So it’s okay if you forget the actors’ and actresses’ names.
However, this review still has many problems as it was being written during an exam.

Addition:
Cruella is not that “masterpiece”, anyway.
