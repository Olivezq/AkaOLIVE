2021 Remix

“是不是生活太艰难，还是活色生香。我们都遍体鳞伤，也慢慢坏了心肠。”
– 朴树《清白之年》

The song above happened to be one of the readings in a  Moke Chinese Exam of a district in our city. Either in Gulou or Qinhuai, I don’t quite remember.
When Mr. Yang was illustrating this reading, I saw my deskmate wrote the lyric high at the top of the paper.
I asked him why, and he shook his head.
But that sentence, which generally means “Is life too hard, or live fresh. We were all bruised, and ill-disposed.”in English, was deeply branded in my mind ever since. Because it was so beautiful and real.

Sun was just shinning bright as usual and we were having our precious PE lessons. That was when I started to feel sad as I lied down on a pad and looked up at the heavy shades of the tree leaves.
I thought about leaving. Leaving this school which I had stayed in for three years, leaving the life I was accustomed to.

The last week in my secondary school, I took a digital record pen along with me. Trying to record everything that would eventually leave me.
I recorded the wind, the chats, the flood when everyone was rushing towards the school canteen, the tones and intonations of the teachers. Sometimes the rain, the noises of fans, the drop of a pen, the fall down of a pile of books. Even the laughters, the silence.

We said goodbye. Almost everyone, Claire, Kevin, Vincent, Apple, Alice, Sun, Laura, Chloe, Cousins, Brian…

The leaving still came and I graduated from secondary school. But another story has only just begun.

Looking back at last year, I will use “leave” to describe 2021 if I can only use one word.

In this year, I graduated, I experienced my best summer holiday ever, I went to an international department, I built up this site, I met new friends. Rewinding and reconsidering, 2021 seemed so unusual.
In 2017, I graduated from primary school. It was a dark period of my life and I was very fortunate to come to my secondary school at that time. The journal I kept in 2017 was still in my wardrobe, written my thoughts in ink. Now, I still keep a journal and everytime I review it, I saw times different from those in 2017.
But that doesn’t mean 2021 is a totally-happy year. We experienced COVID-19 period and had our final exam which had a decisive influence on our future. We had a lot of pressure, suffered pain. I didn’t go to NFLS as I expected.

Time passed fast, like the stream touched across my palm when I washed my hand on the last day of 2021.

As the lyric said at the beginning of this remix.
Life is hard. You suffered a lot to get used to it. You may get cynical, you may get heart-broken.

But as long as you insist on living, accept changes, life is live.
