A/N:
This is the translations I did for these two songs in late April, 2022. I uploaded the translations on Netease Music, so you can find them out by searching the songs’ names and listen to them.
Hamilton is my favorite musical. Alexander Hamilton was my hero and his ambitions really inspired me. Hamilton:Act 1 is a combination of the songs in Hamilton and was made by another fan of Hamilton‘s. I uploaded both the lyrics and translation for this song.
Shape Of You/No Scrubs is a combination of Shape Of You and No Scrubs. It is said that Ed Sheeran copied No Scurbs to make Shape Of You, but people still have different views about this rumour.
I will keep trying to translate more songs, but I think it will be one year later, as I need time to find songs those I like and have not been translated yet.
